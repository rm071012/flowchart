Emergency Surgery

Vitamin K may be used to reverse warfarin effect

Effect may take up to 6 hours

Full reversal of oral anticoagulants with vit K may result in prolonged anticoagulant resistance

Give 5mg vitamin K by slow IV bolus over 3-5 minutes

Cases of MHV to be discussed with Haematologist on case-to-case basis

DO NOT USE FFP TO REVERSE EFFECTS OF WARFARIN
